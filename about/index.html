About Me

Reading time ~1 minute

I’m an experienced iOS engineer with experience in team and product management. I spent 4 years working in mobile advertising and now for the past year I’ve been running my own small consulting company. I have also worked on personal projects throughout the years, everything from backend to the mobile app and advertising that. I also love to work with electronics and connect them with mobile apps (Raspberry Pi thermostats, etc.)

Aside from that, I’m also interested in renewable energy and sustainable living. I believe we do have to take grater care of our future.

My other passions include music, cars, travel, food and leading a healthy lifestyle.
